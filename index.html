    Pixel Diary is a time management project by developer Alex Santagata, combining two things which, taken individually, he feels indifferent towards: spreadsheets and the past.

    On Pixel Diary, you can:

    • Tap on the color keyboard to fill in your day in half-hour increments
    • Long press on the color keyboard (or click "Colors" above) to edit your activities
    • Admire your year thus far

    Pixel diary uses local storage, so your data will not transfer to other devices or browsers. It may also be removed if you clear local storage from your browser.
