Pixel Diary is a time management project by developer Alex Santagata, combining two things which, taken individually, he feels indifferent towards: spreadsheets and the past.

On Pixel Diary, you can:

  • Tap on the color keyboard to fill in your day in half-hour increments
  • Tap Activities, or long-tap on any activity on the color keyboard, to edit your activities
  • Admire your year thus far
  • Export your year as an image to show your friends and family what you were up to for this entire year

Pixel diary uses local storage to store information, so your data will not transfer to other devices or browsers. It may also be removed if you clear local storage from your browser. You can utilize the import/export options to transfer data to other devices.
